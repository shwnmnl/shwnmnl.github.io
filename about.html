Bio

 I'm interested in computational psychiatry and cognitive neuroscience. I hope to develop innovative applications of AI to the study of humans, brains and consciousness. My current research project aims to quantify subjective experience using artificial neural networks to enhance psychiatric symptom detection.

 Outside of research, I enjoy playing the guitar and have been teaching myself web development on the side. After modifying a basic template to host the AutoArchivist, I was motivated to create something from scratch. This site is my first attempt at integrating HTML/CSS/JS. As you can see, I went heavy on the landing page animations (made with Three.js), but I expect to implement more features in the future, so stay tuned!
